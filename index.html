Our Story

About Our School

Sri Rama Model School is located in Bathuva Village, G. Sigadam Mandal, Srikakulam District, Andhra Pradesh. We provide quality English medium education from Nursery to Class 7.

Under the leadership of Principal Sri Butu Lakshmana Rao Garu, our 12 dedicated teachers serve approximately 250 students.

We believe every child deserves quality education. Our school provides a safe, clean and nurturing environment where children grow academically and as individuals.

After first term — covers June to September syllabus. All subjects tested.

Full-year syllabus. Determines class promotion. Certificates awarded.
